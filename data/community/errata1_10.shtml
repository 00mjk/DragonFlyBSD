Errata Page for DragonFly 1.10.x

General note on errata: We do not roll a release CD every time we bump the sub-version. The standard upgrade method is to build from sources. The build manual page gives you information on how to upgrade your system from sources. See the download page for information on tracking changes with cvsup.

1.10.0 Errata

  • The installer will want to install boot blocks on your CD and floppy devices in addition to your hard drive. You have to uncheck those entries before proceeding. This bug was introduced when we redid our disk management layer and caused the installer to mis-identify CD and floppy media for the purposes of setting up its defaults.
  • The installer may have problems installing additional packages on custom-built CDs via the menu option. This is due to a bug in the new pkgsrc bootstrap. Rebuilding the effected package(s) solves the problem.
